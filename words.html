Arctic

Arctic

1. Areas in the North Pole

2. Very cold

Ex: The freezer is arctic.

Attitude

Attitude

1. A way of thinking or feeling about something

2. Sometimes meaning sassy or rude behavior

Ex: Billy had an attitude towards his mom.

Attract

Attract

1. Cause a specific reaction

2. Have a liking to something

Ex: Sally was attracted to Sammy.

Average

Average

1. Typical number or thing in data

2. Add all the things together, then divide by number of things, to get

Ex: The average number of bananas we have is six.

Avoid

Avoid

1. To stay away from something

2. To stop yourself from doing something

Ex: Timmy avoids nuts since he's allergic.
