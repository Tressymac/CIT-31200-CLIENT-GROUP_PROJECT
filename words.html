Arctic

Arctic

1. Areas in the North Pole

2. Very cold

Ex: The freezer is arctic.

Attitude

Attitude

1. A way of thinking or feeling about something

2. Sometimes meaning sassy or rude behavior

Ex: Billy had an attitude towards his mom.

Attract

Attract

1. Cause a specific reaction

2. Have a liking to something

Ex: Sally was attracted to Sammy.

Average

Average

1. Typical number or thing in data

2. Add all the things together, then divide by number of things, to get

Ex: The average number of bananas we have is six.

Avoid

Avoid

1. To stay away from something

2. To stop yourself from doing something

Ex: Timmy avoids nuts since he's allergic.

Ability

Ability

1. The physical or mental power or skill needed to do something

2. Talent, skill, or proficiency in a particular area

Ex: Ben has the ability to train animals.

Absorb

Absorb

1. To take in (knowledge, attitudes, etc.)

2. To engage or engross wholly

Ex: Paper absorbs water.

Access

Access

1. Permission, liberty, or ability to enter, approach, or pass to and from a place or to approach or communicate with a person or thing

2. A way or means of entering or approaching

Ex: The staircase gives access to the top floor.

Accuse

Accuse

1. To charge with a fault or offense

2. To charge with an offense judicially or by a public process

Ex: You can't accuse me of being selfish.

Act

Act

2. The process of doing something

Ex: Timmy acts weird today.
