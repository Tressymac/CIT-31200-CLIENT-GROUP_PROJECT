What is an Idiom?

Lets find out!

An Idiom is

A common expression that has a different meaning than what is being said.

Poem

I've got something to tell you. I think it would be best, although you don't want to hear it, I need it get it off my chest.

I bought a car from a man I knew (whose real name nobody knows). He charged me £2000 for a really old car. I really paid through the nose.

You had to hand it to him, he was a popular business guy, drove a very hard bargain and had a finger in every pie.

I thought I was getting a great deal. I've been making good headway on maths skills but it turned out to be a real rip-off and it's left me with nothing but bills.

And just to make it worse, I crashed. The embarrassment hurts me still. It cost £600 to repair so I had to foot the bill.

Now I don't know what to do and I'm hoping for some advice, or maybe some help with the cost, although the car wasn't worth the price.
